Chocolate cupcake from GFP

Ingredients
    Flour: 175g-all purpose flour
    Cocoa Powde: 50g unsweetened cocoa powder
    Sugar: 200g granulated Sugar
    Baking soda: 1 teaspoon
    Baking powder: As required
    Salt: As required
    Butter: 60g unsalted butter, melted and cooled
    Oil: 80ml vegetable Oil
    Eggs: 2 large Eggs
    Milk: 185ml whole Milk
    Vannilla Extract: 2 teaspoons
    Hot water: 60g boiling water

Instructions

Preheat oven. Line a muffin tin.Mix 1cup flour, 1 cup sugar, 1 cup cocoa powder. Add 500ml of milk, 1 large egg,
and 250g of melted chocolate. Pour batter into liners and bake for 18-20 minutes.Let cool completely before frosting
with chocolate buttercream. Beat 500g butter, 1 cups powdered sugar, and 250g cocoa powder.
Frost cooled cupcakes and decorate with sprinkles or shavings. Enjoy your delicious homemade chocolate cupcakes!
